Plastic Collection Strategy

This chart shows how the weighting parameter α affects the total collected value and travel distance of cleanup vessels. A purely proximity-based strategy (α = 1.0) leads to short paths but misses high-value hotspots. A value-driven strategy (α = 0.0) collects less plastic overall due to long travel times. The optimal balance is achieved at α ≈ 0.8.

Figure: Trade-off between proximity and value-based routing in a dynamic marine environment.
